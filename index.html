Welcome!

/estadistika/ is the Filipino word for Statistics and is the core topic of this blog. My name is Al Asaad -- Data Scientist at NeuralMechanics, Inc. (see my LinkedIn profile) -- and I'm the author. Statistician by training with MSc in Statistics from University of the Philippines Diliman. My research interest is in the field of Bayesian inference for Big Data and its application to deep learning models, both for computer vision and natural language processing. I worked with Dr. Magadia on my thesis entitled Bayesian Autoregressive Distributed Lag via Stochastic Gradient Hamiltonian Monte Carlo.

/estadistika/ will serve as my notes for possible research I want to pursue in the future, and it's main purpose is to share my thoughts about Data Science, Machine/Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence with emphasis on the use of Statistics. Needless to say, everything I post here represents my views and not the opinions of my employer.
